Certified Nurse Assistant Career Training

Certified Nurse Assistants play a pivotal role in healthcare. Most cna's are found in a doctor's office or clinic setting, and perform tasks such as, recording patient's medical history, vital signs, blood draws, and injections. Our certified nurse assistant program is set up to cover a large assortment of topics and include hands-on training in various skill areas.

More than just career training, our CNA Training program is designed to encourage students to begin a career in medical care staffing. Medical staffing entreprenuers can expect to make from $122,000 to $1.5M annually. Acquisition of government contracts can increase entrepreneur earnings dramatically. Let our mentors guide you on the path to business ownership.
