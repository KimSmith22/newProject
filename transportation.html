Non-Emergency Medical & Employment Transportation Entrepreneurs

Non-Emergency Transportation Companies typically exist to take elderly and disabled patients from their homes to routine appointments and non-emergency checkups, then back again. Patients who use non-emergency medical transportation companies are usually on stretchers, wheelchair-bound, or rely on walkers or canes to move from place to place. In almost all instances, these folks are simply unable to use traditional transportation services—like taxicabs or buses—to get from Point A to Point B.
